Modern Era is The Era of Drones

According to a Wall Street Journal report, the history of non-military drone use began in earnest in 2006. Government agencies for disaster relief, border surveillance and wildfire fighting, while corporations began using drones to inspect pipelines and spray pesticides on farms. As unmanned aerial vehicle technology improved in the military sector, those same technological improvements could be used in the private sector. However, you may be surprised to hear that recreational drone use actually only took off (pun intended) very recently, and it’s grown fast.
